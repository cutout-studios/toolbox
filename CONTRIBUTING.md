# `@cutout/jsx` Contribution Guide

**Thinking about contributing?** There are a number of ways you can help!

> [!NOTE]
> By making a contribution, you are agreeing to abide by the
> [**Code of Conduct**](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## **Reporting Issues**

### **Unexpected Behavior**

If you find a bug, please
[open an issue](https://github.com/cutout-studios/jsx/issues)! Include as much
detail as possible in the form. Screenshots, logs, and video captures are super
helpful!

> [!WARNING]
> Security Vulnerabilities are different. Please see
> [./SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

### **Feature Requests**

If you have an idea for a new feature or an improvement to an existing one,
please [open an issue](https://github.com/cutout-studios/jsx/issues) to discuss
it!

## **Submitting Code**

### **VSCode Setup**

Look at the included [`.vscode/extensions.json`](./.vscode/extensions.json) to
review the recommended extensions needed for this project.
